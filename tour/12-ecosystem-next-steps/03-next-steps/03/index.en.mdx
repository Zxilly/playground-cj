# Projects and Examples

The [Cangjie open source repositories](https://gitcode.com/Cangjie) on GitCode contain the standard library and toolchain source code, which serve as authoritative real-world examples of the language in use.

Third-party packages contributed by the community are maintained under the [Cangjie-TPC](https://gitcode.com/Cangjie-TPC) organization, covering areas such as networking, serialization, and data processing.

To connect with other Cangjie developers, contribute to the language, or follow the project roadmap, visit the [community page](https://cangjie-lang.cn) for mailing lists, governance information, and contribution guides.
