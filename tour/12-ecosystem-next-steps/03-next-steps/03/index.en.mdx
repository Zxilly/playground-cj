# Community Resources and Advanced Learning

The best way to dive deeper into Cangjie is to read the official documentation and participate in the community. The official Cangjie documentation covers the complete language specification, standard library APIs, and detailed explanations of advanced features.

The [Cangjie open-source repository](https://gitcode.com/Cangjie) hosted on GitCode contains the compiler and standard library source code, making it an excellent reference for understanding the language's internals. Third-party packages contributed by the community are maintained by the [Cangjie-TPC](https://gitcode.com/Cangjie-TPC) organization, covering areas such as networking, data serialization, and database drivers.

Participating in the community allows you to get help, contribute code, or follow language development. Whether it's submitting issues, contributing code, or sharing experiences, these are all important ways to drive ecosystem growth.
